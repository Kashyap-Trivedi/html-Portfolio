The Best Movies According to Kashyap

My top 3 movies of all-time.

12th Fail

Storyline

The real-life story of IPS Officer Manoj Kumar Sharma and IRS Officer Shraddha Joshi.

3 Idiots

Storyline

Two friends are searching for their long lost companion. They revisit their college
days and recall the memories of their friend who inspired them to think differently,
even as the rest of the world called them "idiots".

Tumbbad

Storyline

A mythological story about a goddess who created the entire universe. The plot revolves
around the consequences when humans build a temple for her first-born.
